It all started with an Apple LCII, Kid Pix on 3.5" floppy and trying to make my brothers laugh. From there, little has changed other than the computer and the software.

I am an ever-growing lover of all things creative. Designer, Web Developer and up-and-coming app developer. I'm versatile and am always looking to stay on top of the daily-changing landscape of tech and design. Whether that's new styles and trends in the design world, new languages in the coding world or anything in between.

With a diverse pallete of skills and a robust client experience spanning global brands and varying markets, my creativity has to be unbiased, timely and flexible.I work cohesively with my clients to establish open communication and rock-solid, unwavering teamwork. My goal is to provide exceptional quality despite the size or scope of the project.

Being a lover of all things creative, I also have experience in tertiary software which allows me to be a well rounded creative asset. Audio creation and editing, video editing, and a casual photographer.

I'm a critical thinker, jokester, open-minded, outgoing and never really know a stranger. I genuinely love people and always approach each project from their perspective, their needs and their vision.

In my downtown you'll find me frequenting the nooks and crannies of Atlanta, honing my skills at a hack-a-thon or tech meetup.

I think we'd have a great time working together and doing some pretty great things.

Lets see what we can create together.
